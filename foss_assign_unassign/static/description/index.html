Assign (or) Unassign Followers


Assign or Unassign Followers helps you add followers to a particular model and to a particular record and you can disable Assign or Unassign Followers feature whenever required.


Highlights


Assign Followers

Using this module, You can assign followers to a particular record.

Un-Assign Followers

You can also remove the assigned followers whenever required.

Advantages

  • Assign or Un-Assign followers module helps you assign followers to a particular record.
  • Assign or Un-Assign followers module helps you remove followers for a particular record.
  • You can enable this feature for a particular model and to a particular record.
  • Option to remove the feature whenever needed by just clicking on the button 'Remove Action'

Benefits

  • Using Assign or Un-assign followers module , You can keep your customers or Sales-Person updated about their Quotation.
  • You can also remove the followers whenever they are not required.

Is this app compatible with Odoo Enterprise as well as Community?

Yes, the app works perfectly well with Odoo Enterprise as well as Odoo Community. Odoo Online (Cloud) does not allow installation of third-party apps and hence this app cannot be installed on Odoo Online.

I need an extra feature in this app. How can I request it?

You can contact us to request a customization in the app or you can write to us at sales@fossinfotech.com

How can I disable this Assign or Unassign Feature ?

Go to Assign / Unassign Followers Form, Click on the button 'Remove Action' to disable.
