After Sales Commissioning

After Sales Commissioning module for Odoo 11. This module allows you to define service engineers to their commissions and generates visits for the respective commissions.

Step 1

Define the commissionable products by clicking on the field 'Can be Commissioned' in the Product variants.

Step 2

Map your stages with the field 'Related Status'.

Step 3

Once the Delivery Order has been validated, Commissioning will be created for each done quantity.

Step 4

Two commissions have been generated for two done quantities.

Step 5

Before the Confirmation of the commissioning, Assign a service engineer.

Step 6

On confirmation of the commissioning, A task (or) visit will be created. By clicking on the 'Visits' Button.
You can easily track the visit and you may also create multiple visits for a commissioning.

Step 7

A Generated Visit.

Step 8

On Completion of the Commissioning, A mail notification will be sent to the service enginner.
You cannnot create visits for a completed commissioning.
