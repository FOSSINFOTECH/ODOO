Sales Revision

Sales Revision module for Odoo 11. you can manage different revisions with templates. Each revision will be stored as template.

Step 1

Create a SO quotation.

Step 2

Click on the 'create revision' which generates a serial number. Go to 'Revision Lines'.

Step 3

Go to 'Revision Lines'.

Step 4

When the revision number is clicked , The list of products under the template will be displayed.

Step 5

To apply the revision to the sale order, Click the apply revision button.
