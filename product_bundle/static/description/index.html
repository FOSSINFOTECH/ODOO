Product Bundle With offer Products.

Product Bundle Module allows you to add free products at the time of creating sale order.

Create Product Bundle in Product Master

In Product Master Form We can add offer products under the selling product.

Create Sale Quotation

Add a selling product in Sale Order Line

There is a Update button for displaying the offer products.

Update Offer Products

Click the Update Button to add the offer products into saleorder line.

The offer product quantity will be multiplied with the selling product Quatity. There is a additional field called Is Offer Product,To denote the offer products only.

Delivery Order

Delivery Order Form also can display the offer products. There is a additional field called Is Offer Product,To denote the offer products only.

Invoice

Invoice Form also can display the offer products. There is a additional field called Is Offer Product,To denote the offer products only.
